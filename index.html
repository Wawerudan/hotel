Welcome to Africana beach and resort

Nestled on the pristine shores of a hidden tropical paradise, Paradise Cove Resort offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Established in the early 1980s by a family passionate about hospitality and nature, the resort has grown from a humble seaside retreat into a world-class destination for vacationers seeking peace, beauty, and luxury.
